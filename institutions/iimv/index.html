About IIM Visakhapatnam

IIM Visakhapatnam was established in 2015 and carries full Institute of National Importance status. Located in Visakhapatnam — one of India's fastest-growing industrial cities — it has strong connections with the Vizag-Chennai industrial corridor, including steel, ports, pharma and IT sectors.
