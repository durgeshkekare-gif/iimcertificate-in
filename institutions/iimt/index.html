About IIM Trichy

IIM Tiruchirappalli is one of the fastest-growing second-generation IIMs. With a 175-acre modern campus and 54 faculty from top IIMs and global universities, it has built a strong reputation in analytics, operations and BFSI. Its Senior Management Programme (10+ years eligibility) is particularly strong for professionals targeting senior leadership in South India.
