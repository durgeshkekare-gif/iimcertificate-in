About IIM Mumbai

IIM Mumbai (formerly the National Institute of Industrial Engineering) is NIRF #6 and holds both AACSB and AMBA — rare for an Indian institution. Located in Powai, Mumbai, it brings proximity to India's financial capital. Their Corporate & Strategic Finance program is directly relevant to Mumbai's BFSI ecosystem.
