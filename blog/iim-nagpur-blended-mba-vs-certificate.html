IIM Nagpur: Blended MBA or PG Certificate — Which to Choose?

Two very different programs, same institution

IIM Nagpur offers two programs for working professionals through the program partner — and they serve fundamentally different needs. Understanding the difference before applying could save you ₹9–12L and 1 year of your time.

Program 1: Blended MBA for Working Professionals

Duration: 2 years. Fee: ~₹12–15L + GST. Mode: Blended (online + weekend campus visits). Credential: Full MBA Degree from IIM Nagpur — not a certificate.

This is the only program in India where you can earn a full MBA degree from an IIM without leaving your job. The credential is a degree, not a certificate — which matters for some employment situations, immigration applications and further education. The 2-year duration and ₹12–15L fee reflect this.

Program 2: PG Certificate Programme in General Management

Duration: 1 year. Fee: ₹3,25,000 + GST. Mode: Online + campus immersion. Credential: PG Certificate from IIM Nagpur + alumni status.

A more accessible, faster and significantly cheaper IIM credential. If your career goal is achievable with a PG certificate from an IIM — and for most professionals in most industries, it is — this is better value.

Decision framework

SituationChoose
You specifically need a degree (visa, promotion policy, further education)Blended MBA
You want IIM credential for career advancementPG Certificate (faster, ₹9–12L cheaper)
You have 3+ years exp and want general management upgradePG Certificate
You want full MBA from IIM while workingBlended MBA — unique in India

If a certificate from a higher-ranked IIM (like IIM Kozhikode at ₹1.95L for Strategic Management) would serve your career goals, that may be better value than either IIM Nagpur program.
