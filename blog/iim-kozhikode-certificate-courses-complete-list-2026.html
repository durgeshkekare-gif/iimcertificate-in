Why IIM Kozhikode is India's best-value IIM for certificates

IIM Kozhikode is ranked NIRF #3 Management — above IIM Calcutta (#7), IIM Indore (#8), and 17 other IIMs. It holds Triple Crown accreditation (AACSB + AMBA + EQUIS) — shared by fewer than 1% of business schools globally, and an FT global ranking of #69. Yet IIMK certificate fees (~₹2L) are significantly lower than IIM Ahmedabad (~₹4–5L) or IIM Bangalore (~₹4L) for comparable programmes. This makes IIMK the highest NIRF-per-rupee IIM certificate in India.
