IIM Calcutta Certificate Programs 2026 — Complete Guide

IIM Calcutta — NIRF #7 Management, Triple Crown accredited

IIM Calcutta is one of India's original three IIMs (along with IIMA and IIMB), established in 1961. NIRF Management ranking #7 in 2025. Triple Crown accreditation: AACSB + AMBA + EQUIS — placing it among the world's top 1% of business schools. Its Joka campus in Kolkata is iconic, and the alumni network spans the most senior positions in Indian industry.

IIM Calcutta SMP — who should apply

The Senior Management Programme (SMP) is IIM Calcutta's flagship for 10–15 year professionals. It targets people in or approaching director/VP/CXO roles. The Joka campus immersion is a significant perk — the IIM Calcutta campus is widely considered India's most beautiful B-school campus, and the networking with a cohort of senior peers is a primary value driver. Best for: Kolkata and east India-based professionals, senior finance and operations executives, PSU senior managers targeting private sector transition.
