IIM Kozhikode Executive Programs 2026 — Which One to Choose

IIM Kozhikode executive programs — overview

IIM Kozhikode (IIMK) offers three executive certificate programs through the program partner in 2026 — covering different career needs at different price points. IIMK is NIRF #10 nationally and AACSB accredited, with a dedicated executive education satellite campus at Infopark, Kochi.

Program 1: New Age Management Programme for Senior Professionals

Duration: 1 year. Mode: Online + campus immersion. Fee: ~₹5–6L + GST. Eligibility: 10+ years of experience minimum.

This is IIMK's senior-level program — designed for professionals with a decade or more of experience who want to broaden their strategic perspective. It covers digital transformation, leadership, new business models and cross-cultural management. Alumni receive IIMK Executive Alumni status.

Program 2: Professional Certificate Programme in Strategic Management

Duration: 1 year. Mode: Online. Fee: ₹1,95,000 + GST (3 instalments: ₹85K + ₹70K + ₹40K). Eligibility: Graduate, any discipline — no minimum experience requirement.

This is IIMK's most accessible program — open to any working professional and priced significantly below the senior program. It covers competitive strategy, business planning, leadership and digital strategy. Strong value for mid-level professionals early in their management career.

Program 3: Professional Certificate Programme in Investment Banking

Duration: 6 months. Mode: Online. Fee: ₹2,40,000 + GST. Target: Finance professionals.

Specialised IIM program focused on investment banking, capital markets, M&A and valuation. Not a general management program — specifically for finance professionals looking to move into or advance within investment banking, corporate finance or capital markets roles.
