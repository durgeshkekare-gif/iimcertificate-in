IIM Ahmedabad Executive Programs 2026 — Which One to Apply For

IIM Ahmedabad executive certificate programs for working professionals 2026 — Accelerated GMP and CIO/CTO program. Fees, eligibility and how to choose.

Why IIM Ahmedabad is different

IIM Ahmedabad has been NIRF #1 in management for 6 consecutive years. It is ranked #27 globally for Executive MBA by the Financial Times 2026 — making it one of very few Indian institutions on the FT global ranking. Its case method pedagogy, faculty quality and alumni network are widely considered the best in India.

The IIM Ahmedabad credential on a CV is immediately recognisable to any hiring manager in India. It signals not just the credential but the selection process — IIM Ahmedabad programs are selective even for executive education.

Programs available through Jaro Education

1. Accelerated General Management Programme (Accelerated GMP)

Duration: 6 months. Fee: ~₹8L + GST. Eligibility: 10+ years of experience, currently in a leadership role. This is a senior program — designed for business heads, VPs and Directors who want to strengthen their general management and strategic thinking toolkit with an IIM Ahmedabad credential.

2. IT Management: Strategic Business Leadership — Future CIO/CTO

Duration: 6 months. Fee: ~₹5.5L + GST. Eligibility: 10+ years in IT, currently in a senior technical leadership role. The only IIM program specifically designed for technology leaders making the transition to C-suite. Covers business strategy, financial management and organisational leadership through a technology lens.

Who should apply?

Both programs are genuinely for senior professionals. If you have 5–7 years of experience, consider IIM Kozhikode or IIM Indore first — the selection criteria and peer cohort at IIM Ahmedabad are calibrated for 10+ year professionals currently in leadership roles. The IIM Ahmedabad credential is most valuable when your application demonstrates that you earned it.

Application process

Application → Profile review by IIM Ahmedabad → Shortlisting → Admission offer. No entrance exam. Your professional experience and current designation are the primary selection criteria. Free counselling through this site can help you assess your profile before applying.
