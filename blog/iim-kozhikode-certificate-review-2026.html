IIM Kozhikode — institution overview

IIM Kozhikode (IIMK) is NIRF #3 in Management — India's third-ranked management institution. Triple-Crown accredited: EQUIS (EFMD), AMBA (UK) and AACSB — one of only a handful of Indian institutions with all three. FT Global MBA Rankings 2025: #69 globally. Established 1997 in Kozhikode, Kerala. IIMK offers two certificate programs for working professionals that are particularly relevant in 2026.

Program 1 — Applied Analytics & GenAI for Business Decisions

This is IIMK's flagship certificate for non-technical professionals wanting analytics and AI skills. Duration: 12 months. Fee: approximately ₹2L + GST. Format: live online weekend sessions with case-based teaching. No coding required — the programme is designed for managers, not engineers. Curriculum covers: social media analytics, predictive analytics, big data fundamentals, business intelligence, machine learning concepts (conceptual), GenAI for business decisions, and data-driven strategy.

This programme is particularly well-suited for: marketing and sales professionals wanting data-driven skills, finance professionals wanting analytics capabilities, operations managers wanting to use data for process improvement, and any senior professional wanting the IIM Kozhikode credential with AI/analytics focus.

Program 2 — Strategic Sales Management & New Age Marketing

Duration: approximately 1 year. Fee: approximately ₹2L + GST. Covers: analytics for sales, digital marketing metrics, customer analytics, attribution modelling, CRM analytics, modern marketing platforms and data-driven campaign management. Designed for sales managers, marketing professionals and brand managers. The "New Age Marketing" component directly addresses AI-powered marketing tools, performance marketing analytics and customer journey analytics.

Is IIM Kozhikode certificate worth ₹2L?

The IIM Kozhikode credential at ₹2L is among the most cost-efficient IIM certificates available — NIRF #3 institution at the same price point as lower-ranked programs. The analytics and GenAI curriculum is directly applicable to most management roles in 2026. Professionals who complete this program and actively leverage the IIM Kozhikode credential in job markets or internal promotion cycles consistently report positive career outcomes within 12–18 months.
